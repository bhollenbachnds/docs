This manual introduces you to **PADS4 Scheduler**, the dedicated end-user application for planning and managing presentation content across your digital signage network.

## What is PADS4 Scheduler?

PADS4 Scheduler allows users to:

- Schedule when and where presentations are displayed  
- Assign content to specific displays, time slots, or events  
- Easily manage changes using a user-friendly calendar-based interface  
- Coordinate dynamic content delivery based on time, location, or data triggers  

Whether you're managing digital signage in a corporate office, airport, retail space, or public venue, **PADS4 Scheduler** gives you the control and flexibility to deliver content exactly when and where it's needed.
