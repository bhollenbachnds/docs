
The **footer properties** define the **bottommost section** of a table element, often used for **special remarks**, **timestamps**, or **summary data**.

---

## **Footer Settings**  

### **Show Footer**  
- **Enable or disable** the footer's visibility.  

### **Edit Footer Content**  
To customize the footer:  

1. Click **Edit** to open the **Edit Footer** window.  
2. Add **page elements** and adjust **properties** as needed.  
3. In the **File menu**, click **Close**.  
4. Click **Yes** to confirm **changes**.  
5. Click **OK** or **Apply** in the **table properties** window to finalize.  

### **Automatic Shrink/Grow**  
- If enabled, the **footer height** adjusts based on its **content size**.  

---

## **Best Practices & Remarks**  

- **Full Design Control:** When editing the footer, you can add **any page elements** (except other table elements).  
- **Dynamic Display:** Use **data providers** to show **real-time content**.  
- **Automatic Shrink/Grow Functionality:**  
  - Adjusts footer height based on the **bottommost element**.  
  - If an **element resizes** or **becomes invisible**, the system recalculates the footer height.  
  - If **all footer elements** disappear, the **footer is hidden**, freeing space for the **table rows**.
