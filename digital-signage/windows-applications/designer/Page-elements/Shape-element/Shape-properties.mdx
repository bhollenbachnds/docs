
These properties define the **type of shape**, its **fill color**, and **background settings**.  

---

## **Shape Settings**  

### **Shape Type**  
- Select from **predefined shapes** such as **rectangle, square, circle, or other geometric forms**.  

### **Fill Color**  
- Choose a **fixed color, gradient, texture, or pattern** to fill the shape.  
- Can be set to **transparent** to create a **frame effect** over other elements like videos.  

### **Background Color**  
- Choose a **fixed color, gradient, texture, or pattern** to fill the **background surrounding the shape**.  

---

## **Tips & Remarks**  
- **Transparent fill colors** allow you to overlay shapes on other elements while keeping visibility.  
- **Background color** applies to the **space around the shape** and can enhance **contrast or layering effects**.
