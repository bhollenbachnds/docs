
These properties define the **position of your element** on the page.

---

### **Position**

| **Property** | **Description**                                      |
|--------------|------------------------------------------------------|
| **Left**     | Horizontal position (in pixels) of your element.     |
| **Top**      | Vertical position (in pixels) of your element.       |

---

### **Remarks**

- If **Snap to Grid** is enabled, you can **manually enter positions** that do **not align to the grid**, allowing precise placement.
- The **position properties update automatically** when:
  - You move the element using the **mouse** or **arrow keys**.
  - You **paste** the element.
  - You apply **formatting functions** to the element.
- **Note**: If your element is **rotated**, the position properties reflect the position of the **unrotated (original) element**.
