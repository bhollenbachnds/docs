
These properties determine the **rotation angle** at which the content of your element is displayed.

---

### **Rotation**

| **Property** | **Description**                                                                                   |
|--------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Angle**    | Set the **rotation angle** (in degrees). <br> You can **select a predefined angle** or **manually enter** any value. |

---

### **Remarks**

- Rotation angles are measured **clockwise**.
- By default, the **center of the element** serves as the **pivot point** for rotation. 
- **Note**: If **automatic sizing** is applied to the element, the pivot point may adjust based on the resized dimensions.
