
These properties control the **direction** and **speed** of the scrolling text, allowing customization for smooth and dynamic movement.

---

## **Scroll Direction & Speed**  

### **Direction**  
Select the desired **scroll direction** from the available options:  
- **Left to Right**  
- **Right to Left** *(default for ticker-style scrolling)*  
- **Top to Bottom**  
- **Bottom to Top**  
- **No Scroll** *(displays static text without movement)*  

### **Speed**  
- Adjust the **speed of scrolling** by using the **slider** or manually entering a **value (milliseconds per pixel)**.  
- A **lower speed value** results in **faster scrolling**.  

---

## **Remarks**  

- **Hardware Performance:** The **smoothness** of scrolling depends on the **PC's hardware and background processes**. Lowering the speed can improve performance, but **perfectly smooth scrolling is not always guaranteed**.  
- **Alignment Adjustments:**  
  - When **scrolling left to right** or **right to left**, **horizontal alignment is ignored**.  
  - When **scrolling top to bottom** or **bottom to top**, **vertical alignment is ignored**.  
- **Static Display:** If **No Scroll** is selected, the text remains **stationary** instead of scrolling.
