
Text elements allow you to display various types of text, from **simple static text** to **dynamic real-time data** with effects like **shadows, rotations, transparency, and smooth transitions**.  

Text elements can be **connected to external data sources** for live updates, making them ideal for **news tickers, real-time information displays, and dynamic messaging**.  

---

## **Properties Overview**  

### **Text Display & Effects**  
- **Alternate Options** – Defines how text items switch in a playlist.  
- **Blinking** – Enables text to blink at a specified interval.  
- **Flip** – Flips the text **horizontally or vertically**.  
- **Font** – Sets the font style, size, and formatting (bold, italic, etc.).  
- **Reflection** – Displays the text with a mirrored reflection effect.  
- **Rotation** – Allows you to rotate the text at any angle.  
- **Shadow** – Adds a shadow effect to improve readability or aesthetics.  
- **Skew** – Skews the text horizontally or vertically.  
- **Transparency** – Controls the opacity of the text.  

---

### **Background & Borders**  
- **Background Color** – Sets a solid, gradient, or patterned background.  
- **Border** – Defines the **border color, width, and radius** for rounded corners.  

---

### **Alignment & Positioning**  
- **Text Alignment** – Sets text to **left, center, right, or justified**.  
- **Size** – Defines the **width and height** of the text element.  
- **Position** – Specifies **exact placement** within the layout.  

---

### **Data Integration & Automation**  
- **Data Provider** – Connects text to an **external data source** (e.g., databases, RSS feeds).  
- **Data Scrolling** – Enables **automatic scrolling** of real-time text data.  
- **Data Source Text Conversion** – Defines how multi-line text is handled.  
- **Play List** – Allows **multiple text items** to be displayed in sequence.  
- **Play List Options** – Controls how text items are played.  
- **Rules** – Applies **conditions and triggers** to modify text behavior dynamically.  
- **Property Bindings** – Links specific properties to **external data sources** for real-time updates.  

---

### **Playback & Visibility**  
- **Default Duration** – Sets the duration of **each text item** before switching.  
- **Default Transition Effect** – Defines **how new text appears**.  
- **Proof of Play** – Logs playback details for tracking purposes.  
- **Visibility** – Determines if the text will be **visible or hidden during playback**.  

---

### **Additional Notes**  
- **Custom Effects** – Combine **shadows, reflections, and transparency** for unique styles.  
- **Real-Time Updates** – Integrate with **live data feeds** for up-to-date information.  
- **Flexible Layout** – Position text anywhere and adjust **size, alignment, and rotation**.
