
These properties allow you to define the **appearance** and **style** of the line element, including its **color**, **thickness**, and **line caps**.  

---

### **Line Properties Overview**  

| **Property** | **Description** |
|--------------|-----------------|
| **Color** | Select the **drawing color** for your line. You can choose a **fixed color**, a **gradient** (two or three colors), a **texture**, or a **pattern**. |
| **Width** | Define the **thickness** of the line in **pixels**. |
| **Line Style** | Choose the **style** of the line, such as **solid**, **dashed**, **dotted**, or other available styles from the drop-down list. |
| **Line Caps** | Set the **style for the start and end points** of the line |

---

### **Key Notes**  

- **Color Flexibility**: Besides simple colors, you can create visually rich lines using **gradients, textures, or patterns**, making lines more dynamic and fitting to your design.  
- **Thickness Control**: Fine-tune the line thickness to emphasize importance or subtlety.  
- **Style Variation**: Line styles (dashed, dotted, etc.) help distinguish different types of lines (e.g., guidelines vs. separators).  
- **End Styling**: **Line Caps** give you full control over how lines terminate, useful for creating **arrows, markers**, or **rounded ends**.  

---

### **Example Use Cases**  

- **Arrowed Lines**: Use **Line Caps** with arrow styles to indicate direction.  
- **Dotted Separators**: Set **Line Style** to "dotted" for section dividers.  
- **Gradient Lines**: Use **Color** with a gradient to add a smooth color transition effect for emphasis.
