
You can easily **navigate** between the **master page** and the **normal pages** of your presentation during design.

---

## To Switch to the Master Page

- Go to the **View menu** and select **Master Page**.  
**OR**  
- Click on the **thumbnail of the master page** in the **Thumbnails window**.

---

## To Return to the Normal Pages of Your Presentation

- Use any of the **navigation buttons** in the **Go To section** of the **View menu** to move to normal pages.

---

## Important Notes

- If your presentation **doesn't contain a master page yet**, you need to **create one** first by clicking **Master Page** in the **Insert section** on the **Page menu**.
