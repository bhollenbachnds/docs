
Page elements that are no longer required can simply be removed.

---

## To Remove One or More Page Elements from the Current Page:

1. **Select** the page elements you want to remove (see *Selecting page elements* for details).
2. Click **Remove Elements** on the **Elements** menu.

---

## Tip:

- If the **Design area** or **Elements window** is active, you can also press the **[Del] key** on your keyboard to delete the selected page elements.

---

## Remarks:

- If you **accidentally removed** page elements, you can always click **Undo** on the **Home** menu to restore the previous situation.
