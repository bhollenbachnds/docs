---
title: "FIDS"
---

PADS4 FiDS is a flight information display system that helps airports manage and present live flight data from one central platform. It is used to keep passengers informed across key areas such as arrivals, departures, check-in, gates, and baggage belts.

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Column>
    The system supports real-time updates, timetable and operational flights, and multilingual information, so airports can quickly reflect changes in schedules, gate assignments, or baggage details. It is designed to reduce passenger confusion, improve wayfinding, and make airport communication easier for staff to manage.

    PADS4 FiDS also fits into the broader PADS4 signage platform, which means flight information can be combined with smart, context-aware content and presentation rules. That makes it a practical solution for airports that want clearer communication and a more efficient passenger experience.
