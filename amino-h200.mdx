title: "Amino H200"
---

This manual provides step-by-step instructions for configuring the Amino H200 device and installing the PADS4 Viewer. It covers initial setup, APK installation and viewer setup.

**Compatible Devices:**

- **Amino H200**

**Minimum requirements**

PADS4 Version Required: **2024.1**\
Firmware Amino: **25.6.2540.10R**

## Configure PADS4 Viewer in Orchestra

Amino is using a central console to manage the devices. Make sure you have an account and download the Generic Android Viewer

1. In your browser open the URL: [https://orchestrate.amino.tv/](https://orchestrate.amino.tv/)
2. On the main page go to \*\*Application **sections.** \*\*
3. Click on **Add Application.**
4. Enter PADS4 Viewer as **Application Name**
5. Upload the **Generic Android Viewer APK**

The viewer is ready for deploying the your device.

## Configure Configuration in Orchestra

1. Navigate to **Configurations**
2. Select **Add Configuration** and provide a name for your Configurations
3. Click **Add** to save the Configurations
4. Open the **Parameters** tab.
5. Select the **Target Version** of the Firmware
6. Click **Save Changes** to apply.
7. Open the **Applications** tab in the configuration view.
8. Click on **Add Action** and select your PADS4 application.
9. Verify the app is listed, set **Action** to **Install**, and select the correct **Version** if applicable.
10. Click **Save Changes** to apply.

## Set auto start

1. Open the **Parameters** tab.
2. Under **Application Settings**, select the PADS4 Viewer app from the **Home App** dropdown.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

## Add PADS4 Viewers to Orchestra

1. Navigate to **Devices** and select **Device list.**
2. Click on **Add Device**.
3. Enter the **Serial Number** of your device.
4. Under **Configuration**, select the configuration you want to use.
5. Once the Viewer comes on-line the player will install.

## Configuring PADS4 Viewer

After installing, the PADS4 Viewer opens. Enter your PADS4 server address in the format`https://<host>:<port>/<domain>/`

- Optionally, enter a custom viewer name.
- Click Start to connect

Wait for connection; a green LED and viewer name indicate success.
