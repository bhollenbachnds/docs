The **Schedules** feature in **PADS4** allows you to **schedule presentations** for specific **Viewers** or **Viewer groups**. Schedules can be **one-time (ad hoc)** or **recurring**, giving you full control over content delivery.  

## **Viewing Schedules**  

- **Multiple Views** – Choose between a **table-style list** or a **graphical timeline**.  
- **Flexible Time Periods** – View schedules by **day, week, workweek, month,** or **timeline**.  
- **Filter Options** – Search for specific **presentations** assigned to a selected **Viewer**.  

---

## **Online /Offline Viewers**  

- A **green dot** next to a Viewer’s name indicates it is **online**.  
- A **red dot** means the Viewer is **offline**.  

---

## **Compare Viewers' Schedules**  

- You can switch to **multi-Viewer selection** to **compare schedules** for different Viewers side by side.  

---

## **Scheduling a New PADS4 Presentation**  

Follow these steps to schedule a **new PADS4 presentation**:  

1️⃣ **Click the** ➕ **New** button on the Schedules page.  
2️⃣ **Select one or more presentations** from your **PADS4 Content** by clicking **➕**.  
   - A **file browser** will open to display available content.  
   - If needed, **upload a presentation** from your local PC.  
   - **Tip:** **Double-click** a presentation to preview it before scheduling.  
3️⃣ **Name the schedule** – By default, the **presentation name** is used, but you can edit it.  
4️⃣ **Assign Viewers or Viewer Groups** – Choose which **Viewers** should display this presentation.  
5️⃣ **Set Schedule Priority** (Optional)  
   - **Higher priority schedules override lower priority ones**.  
   - Priorities can be managed using the **Windows PADS4 Scheduler application**.  
6️⃣ **Specify the Display Period**  
   - Define the **start and end date/time** for the schedule.  
   - Click **Edit Recurrence** to set **daily, weekly, or custom recurrence rules**.  
7️⃣ **Click the "Schedule" button** to finalize the scheduling.
