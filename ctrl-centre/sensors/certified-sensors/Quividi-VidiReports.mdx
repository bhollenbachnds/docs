This guide explains how to integrate **Quividi VidiReports** with **PADS4** to retrieve real-time audience measurement data. For more details on Quividi, visit: [www.Quividi.com](https://www.quividi.com).

---

## **Setting Up the Quividi Connection in PADS4**
To establish a connection between Quividi and PADS4:

1. **Open PADS4 CTRL Center**.
2. Navigate to **Sensors** (If the **Sensors** category is not visible, you may need an **IOT module license**. Contact your PADS4 partner for assistance).
3. Click **New Camera Software** and select **Quividi**.

---

## **Configuring the Quividi Sensor**
1. **Enter the following details**:
   - **Name**: A unique identifier for the sensor.
   - **Hostname (IP Address)**: The IP address where the Quividi VidiReports software is running.
   - **Port**: The port used by Quividi to send audience data.

2. **Save the configuration**.

Once configured, **PADS4 will connect to Quividi VidiReports** and start receiving **real-time audience measurement data**.

---

## **Additional Information**
- **Certified Sensors**: For details on data retrieved from Quividi, refer to the **Certified Sensors** page in PADS4.
- **Data Insights**: Quividi provides detailed **audience analytics**, including:
  - **Age and gender detection**
  - **Mood analysis**
  - **Dwell time**
  - **Impressions and attention span**
- **Automation & Events**:
  - The data retrieved from Quividi can be used to **trigger events** and **adjust content dynamically** in PADS4.
  - Audience analytics can inform **real-time content personalization**.

By following this setup, **Quividi VidiReports seamlessly integrates with PADS4**, allowing you to leverage advanced audience measurement for optimized digital signage.
