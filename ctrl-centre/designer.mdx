---
title: "Designer"
---

With PADS4 Designer, you work in a WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) environment, so the canvas on your screen closely reflects what end users will see on their displays or within smart digital building solutions. Designer is a core component of the platform and is used across modules such as Digital Signage, Workspace, Wayfinding, and FIDS (Flight Information Display Systems).

For more information refer to the PADS4 Designer and CMS.
