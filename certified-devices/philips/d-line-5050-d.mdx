This guide provides step-by-step instructions for **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on **Philips D-line (5050D) displays**.

## **First-Time Use**

Before installation, ensure that the **PADS4 Server is set up and configured properly**.

<Note>
  The minimum **PADS4 Server version** required is **PADS4 Foundation 2023.1**.
</Note>

When turning on the device for the first time, the **Configuration Wizard** will appear. If not, **reset the device** as described in **Resetting the Device**.

### **Resetting the Device**

If the device was previously used, perform a **factory reset** to clear storage and prevent software conflicts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home**, followed by **1 8 8 8** on the **Philips remote control**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Open **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **System Tools → Factory Reset**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Click on **Factory Reset** and confirm by pressing **OK**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After resetting, the **Welcome Screen** will appear. Follow the steps in **Configuring the Device**.

### **Configuring the Device**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    On the **Welcome Screen**, select your preferred **language** and click **Start**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    On the **Security Settings** screen, ensure **SICP Network Port** is **enabled**. _(Other settings can be enabled or disabled as required.)_
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    On the **Network Settings** screen, select and configure the **network type**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    On the **Signage Display Settings** screen, press **Skip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    On the **Clone Media Files** screen, press **Skip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    Press **Finish** to complete the setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **Installing PADS4 Viewer**

Follow these steps to install the **PADS4 Viewer** on **Philips D-line (5050D)**.

### **Checking Device Firmware**

Ensure the **correct firmware version** is installed for full **PADS4 Viewer** functionality.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home 1 8 8 8**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Open **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **About**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Ensure the **Build Number** is **FB01.05** _(required for PADS4)_.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### **Installing PADS4Philips from USB Stick**

The **PADS4Philips.apk** file can be found on the **PADS4 Server** in:

```
https://[PADS4Server]/pads4/apps
```

#### **Installation Steps**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Copy the **PADS4Philips.apk** file onto a **USB stick**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Insert the **USB stick** into the **Philips D-line device**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Open the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home 1 8 8 8**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Select **Apps → File Manager**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Open **mnt → usb_storage** and select **PADS4Philips.apk**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    Select **Install** and then **Open** the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>
