This guide provides step-by-step instructions for **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on **Philips Q-line (3550Q) displays**.

---

## **1. First-Time Use**

Before installation, ensure that the **PADS4 Server is set up and configured properly**.

üìå **Minimum PADS4 Server version required:** **PADS4 Foundation 2021.2**

When turning on the device for the first time, the **Configuration Wizard** will appear. If not, **reset the device** as described in **1.2 Resetting the Device**.

---

### **1.1 Configuring the Device**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    On the **Welcome Screen**, select your preferred **language** and click **Start**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    On the **Security Settings** screen, ensure **SICP Network Port** is **enabled**. *(Other settings can be enabled or disabled as required.)*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    On the **Network Settings** screen, select and configure the **network type**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    On the **Signage Display Settings** screen, press **Skip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    On the **Clone Media Files** screen, press **Skip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    Press **Finish** to complete the setup.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The device will display **CMND & Deploy**, an app supplied by **Philips**. **No configuration is required** for this app.

---

### **1.2 Resetting the Device**

If the device was previously used, perform a **factory reset** to clear storage and prevent software conflicts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home**, followed by **1 8 8 8** on the **Philips remote control**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Open **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **System Tools ‚Üí Factory Reset**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Click on **Factory Reset** and confirm by pressing **OK**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After resetting, the **Welcome Screen** will appear. Follow the steps in **1.1 Configuring the Device**.

---

## **2. Installing PADS4 Viewer**

Follow these steps to install the **PADS4 Viewer** on **Philips Q-line (3550Q)**.

---

### **2.1 Checking Device Firmware**

Ensure the **correct firmware version** is installed for full **PADS4 Viewer** functionality.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home 1 8 8 8**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Open **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **About**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Ensure the **Build Number** is **FB01.10** *(required for PADS4)*.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **2.2 Updating Device Firmware**

To update the **firmware**, follow the **Philips instructions**:

üìå **Firmware update guide:** [Download Firmware Instructions](https://www.dropbox.com/s/8sj2pfo7uv01dd9/xxBDL3550Q%20Latest%20FW.pdf?dl=0)  
üìå **Philips Download Center:** [Download Center](https://www.dropbox.com/s/fje9ge0ao8at6rq/One_Link.xlsm?dl=0)

---

### **2.3 Installing PADS4Philips from USB Stick**

The **PADS4Philips.apk** file can be found on the **PADS4 Server** in:

```
C:\Program Files\Net Display Systems\PADS4
```

Alternatively, download the latest version from **[this link](http://go.pads4.com/?fwlink=1006)** and place it in the **apps directory on the PADS4 Server**.

#### **Installation Steps**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Copy the **PADS4Philips.apk** file onto a **USB stick**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Insert the **USB stick** into the **Philips Q-line device**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Open the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home 1 8 8 8**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Select **Storage ‚Üí USB Drive** and open **PADS4Philips.apk**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Select **Install** and then **Open** the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **4. Troubleshooting**

| **Problem** | **Solution** |
|------------|-------------|
| **Block Remote Control, Turn Off Display, Shutdown Commands Not Working** | Ensure **SICP over Network** is **enabled** and hosted on **port 5000**. |
| **Viewer does not start in full screen** | Check **firmware version** and **update if necessary**. |
| **Device fails to connect over Ethernet** | Updating the **firmware** may resolve this issue. |
| **Navigation bar remains visible** | **Firmware update** required. |
| **Viewer repeatedly asks for content storage permission** | Check if **correct firmware version** is installed. |
| **Device not starting on WOL command** | Ensure **WOL is enabled** and a compatible **power save mode** is selected. |
| **Used valid certificate is not accepted by PADS4Philips app** | Check if **date and time** are correctly configured. |
