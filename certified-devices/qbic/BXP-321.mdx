This guide provides **step-by-step instructions** for installing and configuring **PADS4 Viewer** on **Qbic BXP-321** devices running Android.  

---

## **Before You Start**  

### **Supported Qbic BXP-321 Devices**  

The following **Qbic BXP-321** devices are supported:  

- **BXP-321 (Android OS)**  

**Important:** Ensure your **PADS4 Server** is installed and configured before proceeding.  

---

## **Minimum Requirements**  

**PADS4 Server Version:** **2023.1 or later**  
**Firmware Version:** **Kernel 4.4.167 Build v1.0.1**  

**Ensure your device firmware meets the minimum requirements before proceeding with installation.**  

---

## **Installing PADS4 HTML5 Viewer**  

The **PADS4 Viewer** requires an **Android Launcher** for installation.  

**Download the Launcher:**  
```
http://go.pads4.com/?fwlink=1004
```  

### **Step 1: Enable Android Native Mode**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Power on the **Qbic BXP-321** device.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Navigate to **Settings → Player Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Under **Android System**, enable **Switch to Android Native Mode**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

---

### **Step 2: Download & Install the PADS4 Launcher**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open an **Android browser** (e.g., **Lightning Browser**).  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Enter the **PADS4 Launcher Download URL**:  
    ```
    http://go.pads4.com/?fwlink=1004
    ```  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Click **Download**, then **Allow** if prompted.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Wait **about 1 minute** for the download to complete.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Open **Files → Downloads** and locate the **NDS Android HTML5 Launcher**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    Click **Install** and enable **Allow Unknown Sources** if prompted.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Seven">  
    After installation, click **Open**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

---

### **Step 3: Configure PADS4 Viewer**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Enter the **PADS4 Server Address** when prompted.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click **Start** and select **PADS4 HTML5 Viewer** as the **Home App**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Click **Start** again.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Allow **Modify System Settings** when prompted.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Allow **Access to Media Files** when prompted.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

**Once completed, the launcher will download the PADS4 Viewer software and start automatically.**  

---

## **Upgrading PADS4 Viewer via USB Stick**  

**When a new PADS4 Launcher is available, follow these steps to upgrade.**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **Launcher Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click the **Folder Icon** at the bottom right.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Click **Open Link** and select a **browser**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Click **Download** and wait **1 minute**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Go to **Files → Downloads**, and open the **NDS Android HTML5 Launcher**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    If necessary, **enable Install from Unknown Sources**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Seven">  
    Click **Install**, then **Open** after installation is complete.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

---

## **Adjusting PADS4 Viewer Settings**  

**To adjust settings on the device:**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Click and **hold the left mouse button** until the **Settings Menu** appears.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Change options as needed.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

**Alternatively, you can configure settings remotely using:**  
- **PADS4 CTRL Center**  
- **PADS4 Agent**  

---

## **Opening the Launcher Menu from PADS4 Viewer**  

**To access Android settings from the Viewer:**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Click **Launcher Settings** in PADS4 Viewer.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click the **Gear Icon** at the bottom right.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

**If "Hide Status Bar" is enabled, restart the device to exit Android Settings.**  

---

## **Disabling the Android Navigation Bar**  

**To disable the navigation bar:**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **Viewer Options → Launcher Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click the **Gear Icon** at the bottom right.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Select **System Tools → Disable Navigation Bar**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

**To exit, restart the device by disconnecting the power.**  

---

## **Performing a Factory Reset**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **Settings → Backup & Reset**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Select **Factory Data Reset**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    (Optional) Select **Erase SD Card**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Select **Reset Tablet → Erase Everything**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>
