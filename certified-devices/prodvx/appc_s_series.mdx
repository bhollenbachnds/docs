title: "APPC S-series"
---

This manual provides step-by-step instructions for configuring the ProDVX APPC S-series device and installing the PADS4 Viewer. It covers initial setup, APK installation, autostart configuration, and viewer setup.

**Compatible Devices:**

- **APPC-10SFA**
- **APPC-10SFN**
- **APPC-10SF**
- **APPC-10S**

**Minimun requirments**

PADS4 Version Required: **2024.1**

Firmware ProDVX: **PPC-10N-S-series.R25.ProDVX_013.A25_02**

## Initial Device Configuration

Power on the ProDVX APPC 10SFA device to begin setup.

- On the Welcome screen, **agree** to the Terms and Conditions, then click Start​
- (Optional) Enable or disable ProMGR and click Continue.
- **Enable** the PRODVX API, as PADS4 relies on it for proper operation, then click Continue.​
- **Disable** AutoStart and click Continue.
- Set screen **timeout** to Never and click Continue.
- Click **Reboot** Device to apply changes.

## Installing PADS4 Viewer APK

Download the latest PADS4ProDVXv2.apk from your PADS4 server (e.g., https://[your-server]/[domain]/apps) or official sources, and copy it to a USB stick.

1. Insert the USB drive into the ProDVX device.
2. Open the Files app and select the USB drive.
3. Tap **PADS4ProDVXv2.apk**.
4. Click **Continue** on the warning prompt.
5. Click **Install** to proceed.
6. Once complete, click **Done**.

## Setting Up Autostart

Launch the **AutoStart app** from the App Drawer after installation. Click Start App, select PADS4ProDVXv2, then close AutoStart and reboot the device.

This ensures the PADS4 Viewer launches automatically on boot.

## Configuring PADS4 Viewer

After reboot, the PADS4 Viewer opens. Enter your PADS4 server address in the format`https://<host>:<port>/<domain>/`

- Optionally, enter a custom viewer name.
- Click Start to connect

Wait for connection; a green LED and viewer name indicate success.
