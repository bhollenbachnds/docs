This guide provides step-by-step instructions for **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on the **Iiyama 54 & 60 Series** devices.

## **1. First-Time Use**

Before installation, ensure that the **PADS4 Server is set up and configured properly**.

<Note>
  The minimum **PADS4 Server version** required is **PADS4 Foundation 2025.1**
</Note>

When you turn on the device for the first time, the **Configuration Wizard** should start automatically. If it does not, please **factory reset** the device following the instructions in **1.2 Resetting the Device**.

### **1.1 Configuring the Device**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    On the **Welcome Screen**, select your preferred **language** and click **Start**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    On the **Security Setting Screen**, ensure that **SICP Network Port** is **enabled**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    On the **Network Setting Screen**, select your preferred **network type** and configure it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    On the **Signage Display Settings Screen**, click **Skip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    On the **Clone Media Files Screen**, click **Skip**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    On the **Setup Complete Screen**, click **Finish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **1.2 Resetting the Device**

If the device was used previously, perform a **factory reset** to clear storage and prevent software conflicts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Enter the **Android Menu** by pressing the **Home button**, followed by **1 6 6 8** on the **remote control**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Open **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **System Tools → Factory Reset**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Click on **Factory Reset** and confirm by pressing **OK**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After resetting, the **Welcome Screen** will appear. Follow the steps in **1.1 Configuring the Device**.

---

## **2. Installing PADS4 Viewer**

To install the PADS4 Viewer, enter the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home** followed by **1 8 6 6** on the **Iiyama remote control**.

---

### **2.1 Checking Device Firmware**

Ensure the **correct firmware version** is installed for full PADS4 Viewer functionality.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Enter the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home 1 6 6 8**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Open **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **About**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Verify the **firmware version**:

    - **60 Series:** `m7332_eu-userdebug 11 RP1A.200720.011 eng.nobody.20240921.000102 test-keys`
    - **54 Series:** `t7_an400_arm64-userdebug 11 RQ3A.210805.001.A1 eng.yangpe.20230307.012903 test-keys`
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **2.2 Updating Device Firmware**

To obtain the correct **firmware**, contact **Iiyama Support**.

#### **Firmware Update Steps**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Format a **USB flash drive** in **FAT32**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Download the **firmware file** provided by **Iiyama Support** and **extract** it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Copy the **unzipped firmware file** to the **root directory** of the **USB drive**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Insert the **USB drive** into the **Iiyama display**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Start the **screen**. A message will appear:\
    `"Do not power off the TV during the upgrade. Waiting..."`
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    When prompted, remove the **USB drive**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **2.3 Installing PADS4Iiyama from USB Stick**

The **PADS4Iiyama.apk** is available on the **PADS4 Server** in:

```
C:\Program Files\PADS4\Foundation\Apps  
```

Alternatively, download the latest version from [**here**](https://github.com/PADS4/PADS4-Viewer-for-iiyama-signage-devices/releases/latest).

#### **Installation Steps**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Copy the **PADS4Iiyama.apk** file onto a **USB stick**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Insert the **USB stick** into the **Iiyama display**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Enter the **Android Menu** by pressing **Home 1 6 6 8**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Open **Apps** and launch the **File Manager** app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Navigate to `mnt → usb_storage` and open **PADS4Iiyama.apk**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    Click **Install**, then **Open**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **2.4 Configuring the Device for PADS4Iiyama**

#### **Automatic Startup Configuration**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open **PADS4Iiyama** and click on **System Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Select **Signage Display → Custom App**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Click **Select App**, choose **PADS4Iiyama**, and press **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Enable **Force Restart Custom App**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Press **Back** three times until you return to **PADS4Iiyama**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## **3. Supported Features & Limitations**

| Feature | Description                                                                                 |
| :-----: | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✅       | **Automatic Software Updates** – Updates when the **PADS4 Server** updates.                 |
| ✅       | **Offline Mode** – The device continues running presentations for **7 days** while offline. |
| ✅       | **Supports HTTP & HTTPS**                                                                   |
| ✅       | **Maximum 1 Video Per Page**                                                                |
| ✅       | **Cluster Viewer Mode** – Supported.                                                        |
| ✅       | **Interactive Button, Text, Scrolling Text, Image, Table**                                  |
| ✅       | **PowerPoint & PDF Elements** – _(PowerPoint requires installation on PADS4 Server)_.       |
| ✅       | **Device Management** – Remote control via **PADS4 CTRL Center & PADS4 Agent**.             |
| ✅       | **Wake-on-LAN** – Supported.                                                                |
| ✅       | **Proof of Play Tracking** – Monitors content playback history.                             |

## **4. Troubleshooting**

| **Problem**                                      | **Solution**                                                  |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Remote control commands not working**          | Ensure **SICP over network** is **enabled** on **port 5000**. |
| **Browser not running full screen**              | Check **firmware version** – may be incompatible.             |
| **Navigation bar is visible**                    | Updating firmware may fix this.                               |
| **Repeated permission prompts for file storage** | Check **firmware version** – may be incompatible.             |
