This guide provides step-by-step instructions for **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on **ELO I-Series 2** devices.  

---

## **1. Configuring the ELO I-Series 2**  

This page explains how to configure the **SOC player** to run the **PADS4 HTML5 Viewer**.  

### **1.1 Welcome Screen**  

When you turn on the device for the first time, it will automatically display the **Welcome Screen**.  
- **Press the OK button twice** to proceed.  

---

## **2. Installing the PADS4 HTML5 Viewer Android Launcher**  

**PADS4 requires an Android Launcher to be installed.**  

 **Download the latest PADS4 HTML5 Launcher APK**:  
**[Download PADS4 Launcher](http://go.pads4.com/?fwlink=1004)**  

### **Installation Steps**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Copy the **PADS HTML5 Launcher APK** to a **USB stick**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Insert the **USB stick** into the device.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    On the device, open the **Apk Installer app**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Select **Install** and browse the **USB storage** for the **APK file**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    When prompted with **"Do you want to install this application?"**, press **Next** until the **Install** button appears.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    Press **Install** to complete the installation.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Seven">  
    After installation, press **Open**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Eight">  
    The **PADS4 HTML5 Launcher app** will start. Enter the **PADS4 Server location**, specifying:  
    - **Domain name**, **IP address**, or **PC name** of the **PADS4 Server**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Nine - *Optional*" >  
    If **default server settings** were changed, open **Advanced Settings** to enter:  
    - The **correct port number**.  
    - The **host/root path**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Ten">  
    Press **Start**.  
    - The first time the app runs, it will prompt you to **select your Home App**.  
    - Select **PADS4 HTML5 Launcher** and **choose Always**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

**Upon completion:**  
- The **launcher will download the PADS4 HTML5 Viewer software** and start automatically.  
- A **connection indicator icon** will appear at the **bottom-left corner** of the screen.  
  - **Green**: Valid connection.  
  - **Red**: Connection issue (check logs in **Viewer Settings**).  

<Warning>  
A **valid server connection** is required during **first-time setup** to download the **PADS4 HTML5 Viewer software**.  
</Warning>  

---

## **3. Upgrading the PADS4 Launcher from a USB Stick**  

To update the **PADS4 Launcher**, follow these steps:  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Copy the **new PADS HTML5 Launcher APK** to a **USB stick**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Insert the **USB stick** into the device.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Open **PADS4 Viewer Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Select **Launcher Settings** from the menu.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Click the **folder icon** in the **bottom-right corner**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    Navigate to the **USB storage** folder and select the new **APK file**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Seven">  
    When prompted with **"Do you want to install an update to this existing application?"**, press **Install**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Eight">  
    After installation, the **PADS4 HTML5 Launcher** will **automatically start**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

---

## **4. Adjusting PADS4 Viewer Options**  

**On-Device Configuration**  
- **Press and hold the left mouse button** until the **menu appears**.  

**Remote Configuration**  
- Settings can also be changed using **PADS4 CTRL Center** or **PADS4 Agent**.  

---

## **5. Accessing the Android Settings Menu**  

To open the **Android Settings Menu**:  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **Viewer Menu**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click **Launcher Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Click the **gear icon** in the **bottom-right corner**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

<Warning>If **"Hide Status Bar"** is enabled, the only way to exit Android Settings is to **restart the device** by disconnecting power.</Warning>  

---

## **6. Disabling the Android Navigation Bar**  

To prevent access to the **Android Navigation Bar**:  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **Viewer Options**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click **Launcher Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Click the **gear icon** in the **bottom-right corner**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Select **System Tools**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Click **Disable Navigation Bar**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

<Warning>After disabling, the **only way to exit** is to **restart the device** by disconnecting power.</Warning>  

---

## **7. Performing a Factory Reset**  

To reset the device:  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **Android Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Select **Backup & Reset**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Choose **Factory Data Reset**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four - *Optional*" >  
    Select **Erase SD Card**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Tap **Reset Tablet**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    Confirm with **Erase Everything**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>

## **8. Supported Features & Limitations**  

| Feature | Description |  
|:------:|------------|  
| ✅ | **Automatic PADS4 Software Updates** – The HTML5 Viewer updates automatically. |  
| ✅ | **Offline Mode** – The device will continue running presentations and schedules for **7 days** while offline. |  
| ✅ | **Portrait Mode, HTTP/HTTPS Support, Cluster Viewer Mode** |  
| ❌ | **Turn Off Display** – Not supported. |  

### **Playback & Content Support**  

| Feature | Description |  
|:------:|------------|  
| ✅ | **Supports 4x HD (30fps) or 2x HD (60fps) Video Playback** |  
| ✅ | **Streaming Video, Video Rotation, Seamless Playback, Speed Adjustment** |  
| ✅ | **Supports JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF, Text, Tables, Shapes, Scrolling Text, Flash, PDF (Xpert License required)** |  
| ❌ | **Mouse Cursor & Interactive Data Filter Not Supported** |  

### **Device Management**  

| Feature | Description |  
|:------:|------------|  
| ✅ | **Remote Device Management via PADS4 CTRL Center & Agent** |  
| ✅ | **Reboot, Restart Viewer, Shutdown, Wake-on-LAN** |  
| ✅ | **Retrieve Screenshots, Logs, Install Fonts, Proof of Play** |
