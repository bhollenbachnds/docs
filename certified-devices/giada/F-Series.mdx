This guide provides information on **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on **Giada F-Series computers** running **Windows 10 or Windows 11**.  

---

## **1. Compatibility**  

✅ **Supported Operating Systems:**  
- **Windows 10**  
- **Windows 11**  

✅ **Supported Devices:**  
- **Giada D6-Series Computers**  

---

## **2. Installation Instructions**  

To install **PADS4 Viewer** on a **Giada F-Series** device, follow the standard **Windows installation process**.  

<Note>For **detailed step-by-step instructions**, refer to the **[Installing PADS4 on Windows](#)** guide.</Note>

---

## **3. Additional Configuration**  

After installation, configure **PADS4 Viewer** using **PADS4 CTRL Center** or **PADS4 Agent** for:  
- **Network Configuration**  
- **Content Library Setup**  
- **Viewer Settings Adjustments**  

For remote management, use **PADS4 CTRL Center** to:  
- **Monitor Viewer Status**  
- **Manage Proof of Play Logs**  
- **Perform Software Updates**  

---
