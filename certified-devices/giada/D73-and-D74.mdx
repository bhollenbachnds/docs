This guide provides step-by-step instructions for **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on **Giada DN73 & DN74 devices running Android 7.1**.

---

## **1. First-Time Use**

During installation, you will need to **configure the PADS4 Server location**. It is recommended to **complete the PADS4 Server installation first** before proceeding with device setup.

<Note>
   The minimum **PADS4 Server version** required for the **PADS4Giada app** is **PADS4 Foundation 2022.1**.\
  The minimum **PADS4Giada version** supporting **DN73 and DN74** is **PADS4Giada 1.0**.
</Note>

---

### **1.1 Resetting the Device**

If the device was previously used, a **factory reset** is recommended to **clear storage** and **prevent interference** with the **PADS4 Viewer software**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open **Android Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Select **Backup & Reset**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **Factory Data Reset**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Click **Reset Tablet**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Confirm by clicking **Erase Everything**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **1.2 Updating Firmware**

Ensure the **Giada DN73 & DN74** devices are running **PADS4-supported firmware versions** before installing the **PADS4Giada app**.

#### **Supported Firmware Versions**

| Device   | Firmware Version | Loader Version | Chip Info | Android Tool Version | Download          |
| -------- | ---------------- | -------------- | --------- | -------------------- | ----------------- |
| **DN73** | 7.1.00           | 2.44           | RK322H    | 2.38                 | [**Download**](#) |
| **DN74** | 7.1.00           | 2.45           | RK330C    | 2.63                 | [**Download**](#) |

---

### **1.3 Enabling Default Superuser Access**

This step is **required** to enable **automatic updates** of the **PADS4Giada app**. If **Superuser Access** is not granted, **manual interaction** will be required for updates.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **SuperSU app**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Go to **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Click **Default Access**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Select **Grant** in the dialog that appears.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Exit the app.
  </Step>
</Steps>

_Optional:_ To reset **screen orientation**, navigate to:\
➡ **Android Settings** → **Display** → **HDMI** → **Screen Rotation** → **Select "0"**.

---

## **2. Installing PADS4 Viewer**

Follow the steps below to **install and configure PADS4 Viewer** on **Giada DN73 and DN74**.

---

### **2.1 Installing PADS4Giada from USB Stick**

The **PADS4Giada.apk** is available on the **PADS4 Server** in the following directory:

```
C:\Program Files\PADS4\Foundation\Apps
```

Alternatively, download the **latest version** from [**here**](https://github.com/PADS4/PADS4-Viewer-Giada/releases/latest) and place it in the **PADS4 Server apps directory** to ensure the correct version is installed.

#### **Installation Steps**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Copy the **PADS4Giada.apk** to a **USB stick**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Insert the **USB stick** into the **Giada DN73/DN74 device**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Open the **File Explorer app** on the device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Select **USB Storage**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    Click on the **APK file** to install.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    If prompted with a **security warning**, click **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Seven">
    Enable **Unknown Sources** and confirm by clicking **OK**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Eight">
    Return to **File Explorer** and click the **APK file** again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Nine">
    Click **Install** in the confirmation dialog.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Ten">
    After installation, click **Open** to start the **PADS4Giada app**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **2.2 Validating Date & Time Configuration**

Ensure the **date and time** are set correctly to avoid **certificate errors** or **connection issues**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **PADS4Giada app**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two">
    Click **System Settings** to open **Android Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Navigate to **Date & Time**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Configure the **correct date and time**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five - *Optional*">
    Enable **Auto Sync Date & Time** if supported by the network.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **2.3 Connecting to PADS4 Server**

**PADS4 Server Address Format:**

```
protocol://host:port/hostpath/  
```

- **Protocol:** `HTTP` or `HTTPS` (depends on SSL certificate).
- **Host:** DNS name or **IP address** of **PADS4 Server**.
- **Port:** _(Optional - specify only if using non-default ports)._
- **Host Path:** Configured in **System Management** _`(default: PADS4)`_.

#### **Connection Steps**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Enter the **PADS4 Server Location**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Two - *Optional*">
    Specify a **Viewer Name** (if left blank, PADS4 will generate one).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Three">
    Press **Start**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Four">
    Grant **permission to store content** when prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Five">
    If **Android System WebView** is outdated, it will be updated automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Six">
    The **PADS4 Viewer** will be **downloaded and installed**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Step Seven">
    If prompted, set **PADS4Giada as the Home App** and press **Always**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

### **2.4 Adjusting PADS4 Viewer Settings**

To access **Viewer Settings**:

- **Press and hold the left mouse button** or **touch the screen for 3 seconds**.

---

### **2.5 Updating PADS4 Viewer & PADS4Giada App**

The **PADS4 Viewer & PADS4Giada app** will **automatically update** when the **PADS4 Server** is updated.

---

### **2.6 Accessing Android Menu**

If you access the **Android Menu** while **PADS4Giada** is running, the device will **reboot in 30 seconds** (as the **Viewer Monitor Service** detects inactivity).

To prevent reboot, **stop the PADS4Giada app within 30 seconds**.

## **3. Supported Features & Limitations**

Below are the supported features and limitations for **PADS4 Viewer on Giada DN73 & DN74 (Android 7.1).**

---

### **3.1 General Device Capabilities**

| Feature | Description                                                                                                 |
| :-----: | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✅       | **Supported Display Models** – Compatible with DN73 & DN74 devices.                                         |
| ✅       | **Portrait Mode** – Supported.                                                                              |
| ❌       | **Remote Control Blocking** – Only blocks buttons on the device and touch screen (not full remote control). |
| ✅       | **Supports HTTP & HTTPS**                                                                                   |

---

### **3.2 Playback & Content Support**

| Feature | Description                                                                        |
| :-----: | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✅       | **Maximum Videos Supported** – Up to **2 simultaneous videos**.                    |
| ❌       | **Streaming Video** – Not supported.                                               |
| ✅       | **Image Formats** – JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF                                             |
| ✅       | **Text, Line, Shape, Scrolling Text, Formatted Text, Tables, Interactive Buttons** |
| ❌       | **Flash Element** – Not supported.                                                 |
| ✅       | **Web Element** – Interactive **Address Bar not supported**.                       |
| ✅       | **Colors** – Supported (except for **texture and pattern colors**).                |

---

### **3.3 Data Connectivity & Integrations**

| Feature | Description                                                                                                |
| :-----: | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✅       | **Data Providers** – Supports **70\+ PADS4 data providers** for retrieving and integrating real-time data. |
| ✅       | **Viewer Variables** – Simplifies content management without modifying presentations.                      |
| ✅       | **Property Bindings** – Allows dynamic element properties using external data sources.                     |

---

### **3.4 Rules & Automation**

| Feature | Description                         |
| :-----: | ----------------------------------- |
| ✅       | **Presentation Rules** – Supported. |
| ✅       | **Page Rules** – Supported.         |
| ✅       | **Element Rules** – Supported.      |

---

### **3.5 Device Management**

| Feature | Description                                                                                  |
| :-----: | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| ✅       | **Remote Device Management** – Managed via **PADS4 CTRL Center & PADS4 Agent**.              |
| ✅       | **Connection State Monitoring & Alerts** – Sends alerts for network failures.                |
| ✅       | **Reboot & Restart Viewer** – Supported.                                                     |
| ❌       | **Shutdown & Wake-on-LAN** – Not supported.                                                  |
| ✅       | **Turn Off Display** – Supported.                                                            |
| ✅       | **Screenshot Capture** – Retrieve screenshots of the display.                                |
| ✅       | **Logs & System Monitoring** – Viewer logs, synchronization logs, and system info available. |
| ✅       | **Viewer Settings & Font Installation** – Manage settings and install custom fonts.          |
| ❌       | **LED Light Control** – Not supported.                                                       |
| ✅       | **Proof of Play Tracking** – Monitors content playback history.                              |
| ✅       | **Blackbox Support** – Provides system logs for troubleshooting and support.                 |

---

## **4. General Limitations**

<Warning>
   **Data Providers** – **HTML5 Viewer** cannot read data directly from external sources; it must be retrieved through the **PADS4 Data Server**.
</Warning>

<Warning>
   **Content Library** – Supports **content from PADS4 Content Library** but **does not support file paths pointing to a local disk**.
</Warning>

<Warning>
   **Proof of Play Storage Limit** – Stores **up to 50,000 records** offline. When the limit is reached, the **oldest records are automatically deleted**.
</Warning>

<Warning>
   **Storage Capacity** – The available **storage for content varies** depending on the **device and browser**.
</Warning>
