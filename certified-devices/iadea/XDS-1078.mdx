This guide provides step-by-step instructions for **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on the **IAdea XDS-1078 (Android 12)** device.  

---

## **1. First-Time Use**  

Before installation, ensure that the **PADS4 Server is set up and configured properly**.  

<Note> The minimum **PADS4 Server version** required is **2023.1**.</Note>  

---

### **1.1 Resetting the Device**  

If the device was previously used, perform a **factory reset** to clear storage and prevent software conflicts.  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **IAdea Basic Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click on **Advanced**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Select **System**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Click on **Factory Default**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Click **OK** to confirm.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

<Warning> **Factory resetting will erase all data**. Please back up any important information before proceeding.</Warning>  

---

### **1.2 Checking Device Firmware**  

Ensure that the **firmware is updated** to at least **4.0.3 or higher** before installing PADS4.  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **IAdea Basic Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click on **Advanced**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Select **Information**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Check the **firmware version** displayed on the right-hand side.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

<Note> Required firmware version: **4.0.3 or higher**.</Note>  

---

### **1.3 Updating Firmware**  

If your firmware version is outdated, follow these steps to update:  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **IAdea Basic Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click on **Advanced**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Select **System**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Click **Firmware Update**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Click **Check Latest Firmware from Internet**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    If an update is available, select **Update from Internet**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

<Note> **Firmware updates may take some time to complete.**</Note>  

---

### **1.4 Setting Date & Time Configuration**  

Incorrect date/time settings may cause **certificate errors and connection issues**.  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **IAdea Basic Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click on **Time Zone**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Adjust the **Date & Time** settings if necessary.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Click the **Back button** to save changes.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

---

## **2. Installing PADS4 Viewer**  

Follow the steps below to install and configure **PADS4 Viewer on the IAdea XDS-1078 (Android 12)**.  

---

### **2.1 Downloading PADS4 Viewer**  

The **PADS4IAdea.apk** file can be downloaded from:  

- **PADS4 Server**:  
  ```  
  https://[yourPADS4Server]/[domain]/apps  
  ```  
  *(Replace `[yourPADS4Server]` with your server’s IP or domain name. The default domain is `PADS4`.)*  

- **APP Center**: Download the latest version from **[here](#)**.  

<Warning> If you download the latest version, ensure that you also place it in the **PADS4 Server apps directory**, as the device will install the version available on the server.</Warning>  

---

### **2.2 Installing PADS4 Viewer**  

#### **Accessing the Android Home Screen**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **IAdea Basic Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click on **Advanced**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Tap **three times** on the **lower right corner** of the screen.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    The **Android Home Screen** will now be accessible.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

#### **Installation Steps**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Copy the **PADS4IAdea.apk** onto a **USB stick**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Insert the **USB stick** into the **IAdea XDS-1078** device.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Open the **Files app** on the device.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Click on **USB Disk**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    Select **PADS4IAdea.apk** and click **Continue** in the dialog that appears.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    Click **Install** when prompted.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Seven">  
    Wait for installation to complete and click **Done**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

---

### **2.3 Setting PADS4 Viewer to Start Automatically**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **IAdea Basic Settings**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Click on **Advanced** → **Content**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Click on **AppStart**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four">  
    Click on **(+) Advanced**.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Five">  
    In **Package Name**, enter:  
    ```  
    com.pads4.viewer.iadea  
    ```  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Six">  
    Click **Set** to save the setting.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Seven">  
    Restart the device or click **Play** to start **PADS4 Viewer**.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>

## **3. Supported Features & Capabilities**  

### **PADS4 Element Support**  

| Feature | Description |  
|:------:|------------|  
| ✅ | **Text, Formatted Text, Images** *(JPG, PNG, BMP, GIF up to 1920x1080)* |  
| ✅ | **Video Playback** *(H264, H265, WebM VP8 up to 1920x1080, up to 2 FHD videos simultaneously)* |  
| ✅ | **Streaming Video** *(HTTPS & HLS streaming supported)* |  
| ✅ | **Web, Table, PowerPoint, PDF Elements** |  

---

### **Device Management Features**  

| Feature | Description |  
|:------:|------------|  
| ✅ | **Remote Device Management** – Managed via **PADS4 CTRL Center** |  
| ✅ | **Restart Viewer & Restart Device** |  
| ❌ | **Shutdown Device** – Not supported |  
| ✅ | **TLS HTTPS Support** |  
| ❌ | **Wake-on-LAN** – Not supported |  
| ✅ | **Proof of Play Tracking** |  
| ✅ | **Content Caching & Offline Schedules** |  
| ❌ | **NFC/RFID** – Not supported |
