To access the Crystal platform:

1. **Open your browser**  
   Crystal supports modern web browsers such as Firefox and Chrome. We recommend saving the login page to your bookmarks for quick access in the future.

2. **Enter the provided URL**  
   This will be shared by your administrator. Type it directly into the browser’s address bar.

3. **Log in using your credentials**  
   Use the ID and password given to you. These credentials are typically provided during onboarding. If you're unsure about your login information, please contact your administrator.

4. **Select the ‘Digital Signage’ module**  
   Once logged in, the homepage will display accessible modules. Click on “Digital Signage” to begin managing or creating your content.
