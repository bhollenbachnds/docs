To begin using PADS4 CMS Designer, navigate to the web interface using the URL provided by your system administrator.

## Enter Your Credentials

1. Open a web browser and enter the address of your PADS4 CMS instance.
2. On the login page, enter your **username** and **password**.
   - These credentials are usually provided by your IT department or system administrator.
3. Click **Login** to proceed.

> If Single Sign-On (SSO) is enabled, you may also have the option to sign in using your organization's credentials.

## Select Digital Signage

After logging in, you will be presented with available PADS4 modules.

1. From the module selection screen, choose **Digital Signage** to access the CMS Designer environment.

This will direct you to the main homepage of the CMS where you can begin managing categories, creating templates, and publishing content.
