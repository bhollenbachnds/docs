The **Equipment** tab allows administrators to define equipment items that can be associated with rooms in the **PADS4 Workspace Module**. This helps end-users understand what tools or devices are available in each space.

![Equipment Management Screen](/mnt/data/image.png)

## Adding Equipment

To add a new piece of equipment:

1. Click the **Add Equipment** button.

2. In the **Create Equipment** window, fill in the following details:

   - **Name**  
     Enter the name of the equipment (e.g., Projector, Whiteboard, Video Conferencing System).

   - **Image**  
     Upload an image that will visually represent the equipment in the user interface.

![Create Equipment Screen](/mnt/data/image.png)

3. Click **Save** to complete the process.

Once added, the equipment can be assigned to individual rooms through the room configuration panel.
