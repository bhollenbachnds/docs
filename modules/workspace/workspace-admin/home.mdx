## Available Metrics & Insights

- **Defective Equipment**  
  View all reported issues related to defective equipment within the workspace environment.

- **Room Occupancy**  
  Displays the real-time status of rooms and spaces—whether they are free or booked—within the PADS4 Workspace module.

- **Ghost Meetings**  
  Identifies meetings that were reserved but had no check-in, helping to reduce wasted space and improve room usage.

- **Occupancy Rate**  
  Shows the overall occupancy percentage, allowing for data-driven space management decisions.
