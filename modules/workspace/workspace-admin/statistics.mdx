The **Statistics** tab provides reporting and analytical data on room usage within the **PADS4 Workspace Module**. Reports can reflect both **live data** and **historical data**, helping administrators evaluate room utilization and optimize workspace efficiency.

![Statistics Reporting Screen](/mnt/data/image.png)

## Report Types

The dashboard includes various charts and metrics such as:

- **Workspace Occupancy Rate**  
- **Non-Bookable Room Occupancy Rate**  
- **Distribution of Workspace Occupancy Bookings**  
- **Distribution of Workspace Occupancy Duration**

## Exporting Reports

To export data from the dashboard:

1. Select the **dropdown menu** next to the filter or date selection options.
2. Choose **Export** to download the report.
