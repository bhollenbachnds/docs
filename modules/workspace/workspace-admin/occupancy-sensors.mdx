The **Occupancy Sensors** tab allows administrators to view and manage all connected gateways, sensors, and LED devices used in the **PADS4 Workspace Module**.

## Gateways  
Displays all connected **sensor gateways** along with their connection status and activity details.

## Sensors  
Shows all connected **occupancy sensors** and allows administrators to assign them to specific rooms within the system.

## LEDs  
Lists all connected **LED devices** managed by the PADS4 Workspace Module, typically used for signaling room status or availability.
