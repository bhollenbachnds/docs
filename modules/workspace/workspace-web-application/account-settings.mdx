The **Account Settings** section allows you to manage personal preferences and basic information related to your PADS4 Workspace account.

---

### **Accessing Account Settings**

From the **main menu**, click on **Settings** to open your account preferences.

---

### **What You Can Update**

Within the settings screen, you can view or modify the following:

- **Login Information**  
  (Visible, but not editable if using SSO)

- **Password**  
  (Only changeable if youâ€™re not using Single Sign-On)

- **Language**  
  Choose your preferred interface language

- **Notifications**  
  Enable or disable in-app and email notifications

> **Note:** If you are logged in via **SSO**, login credentials and password fields will be disabled for security and compliance reasons.
