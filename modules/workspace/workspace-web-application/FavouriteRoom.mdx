---
title: "Favourite Room"
description: "Mark the rooms and desks you use most often as favourites so they appear at the top of your list in Workspace."
---

Favourites give you quick access to the rooms and desks you use most — for example, the meeting room next to your desk or your preferred workstation. You can mark as many rooms and desks as you like.

## Mark a room or desk as favourite

1. Search for the room or desk you want to favourite.
2. Hover over the result. A white star appears next to it.
3. Select the star. It turns yellow, and the room or desk moves to the top of your list, ready to book.

<Frame>
  ![Marking a room as favourite in Workspace](/images/Workspace/WorkspaceApp/Favourite.png)
</Frame>

<Tip>
  To remove a favourite, select the yellow star again. The star turns white and the room or desk returns to its normal position in the list.
</Tip>
