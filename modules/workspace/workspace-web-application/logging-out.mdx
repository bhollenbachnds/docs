When you're finished using PADS4 Workspace, it’s important to log out—especially on shared or public devices—to ensure your account remains secure.

---

### **How to Log Out**

1. Click on your **user icon** located in the **top-right corner** of the screen.  
2. Select **Log Out** from the dropdown menu.

You will be securely signed out of the application and redirected to the login screen.
