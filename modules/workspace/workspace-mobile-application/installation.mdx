This guide provides step-by-step instructions for installing and configuring the **PADS4 Workspace** mobile application on **Apple iOS** and **Android** devices.

---

## Pre-requisites

Before installation, ensure the following requirements are met:

- A valid **Apple ID** (for iOS) or **Google Account** (for Android) signed in on the device  
- An active **Single Sign-On (SSO)** account  
- **Internet** or **local network** connectivity via WiFi or mobile data  

---

## Installing the Application

To install the **PADS4 Workspace** app:

1. Open the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play Store** (Android)  
2. Search for:  
   ```
   PADS4 Workspace
   ```
3. Select the app and tap **Download** or **Install**

---

## Connecting to PADS4 Workspace

Once installed, you’ll need to connect the app to your organization’s PADS4 Workspace environment:

1. Launch the **PADS4 Workspace** application
2. Enter your organization's PADS4 instance URL, e.g.:  
   ```
   https://yourservername.domain.com
   ```
3. Tap **Save**

---

## Logging in with SSO

To access your account:

1. On the login screen, tap **Single Sign-On (SSO)**
2. Enter your SSO credentials  
   (e.g., your company email address and password)

Once authenticated, you will be logged in and ready to use the app.

---

## Workspace Mobile Interface

After successful login, the mobile app will display the PADS4 Workspace interface, allowing you to:

- Search and book available rooms
- View your reservations
- Receive notifications and more
