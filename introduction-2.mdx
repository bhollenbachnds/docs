title: "Introduction"
---

PADS4 includes a native integration with Canva, enabling users to directly access and use Canva designs within their digital signage presentations. This integration allows designers to leverage Canva’s extensive design capabilities while maintaining centralized content management and scheduling within PADS4.

To use this functionality, a connection must be established between Canva and PADS4 using a secure authentication process. Once connected, users can browse, select, and update Canva designs directly from within PADS4 Design, streamlining the content creation workflow.
