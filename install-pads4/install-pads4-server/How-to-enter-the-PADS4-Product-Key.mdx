## **Activating PADS4 with a Product Key**  

To activate your **PADS4 product**, follow the steps below using the provided **PADS4 Product Key**.  

---

## **Step 1: Open PADS4 CTRL Center**  

1. After installing **PADS4**, launch **PADS4 CTRL Center**.  
2. For additional assistance, refer to the **[PADS4 CTRL Center Help](#)** page.  

---

## **Step 2: Installing the PADS4 Product Key**  

<Steps>  
  <Step title="Step One">  
    Open **PADS4 CTRL Center** and navigate to **Licenses** from the left-hand menu.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Two">  
    Enter the **Product Key** provided by your PADS4 partner.  
    - If additional licenses have been added to your current Product Key, you may continue the process using the existing key.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Three">  
    Click the **"Activate"** button at the top to activate or reactivate the license.  
  </Step>  
  <Step title="Step Four - *Offline Activation*">  
    If an internet connection is unavailable, an **offline activation** method will be displayed.  
    - Follow the instructions provided on-screen.  
    - After receiving the **License File**, click **"Upload License File"** and select the file.  
    - Complete the activation process by following the wizard.  
  </Step>  
</Steps>  

---

Once activation is complete, your **PADS4 installation is ready for use.**
