This guide provides step-by-step instructions for **installing and configuring PADS4 Viewer** on the **IAdea XMP-8550 / XMP-8552 (Android 12)** device.

***

## **1. First-Time Use**

Before installation, ensure that the **PADS4 Server is set up and configured properly**.

<Note>
  The minimum **PADS4 Server version** required is **PADS4 Foundation 2023.1**.
</Note>

When you turn on the device for the first time, it will display the **Basic Settings** screen. ***(If not, reset the device as described in section 1.2 Resetting the Device.)***

***

### **1.1 Prerequisites**

Before installing **PADS4IAdea** on the **IAdea XMP-8550 / XMP-8552**, ensure the following conditions are met:

* **OS:** Android 12
* **Firmware Version:** `4.0.2-48`
* **Network Connectivity:** Device must be connected to a network

***

### **1.2 Resetting the Device**

If the device was used previously, perform a **factory reset** to clear storage and prevent software conflicts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **Basic Settings** on IAdea.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Two">
    Click on **Advanced**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **System → Factory Default**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Four">
    Press **OK** to start the factory reset.
  </Step>
</Steps>

After resetting, the **Basic Settings screen** will appear.

***

### **1.3 Checking Device Firmware**

To ensure full **PADS4 Viewer compatibility**, verify that the device is running firmware version **4.0.2-48**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **Basic Settings** menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Two">
    Click on **Advanced**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Three">
    Select **Information**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Four">
    The **Firmware Version** is displayed on the right side of the screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

### **1.4 Updating Device Firmware**

If the firmware is outdated, update it following **IAdea's official instructions**.

Firmware updates are available on the [**IAdea Support Page**](https://support.iadea.com/).

***

## **2. Installing PADS4 Viewer**

Follow the steps below to install and configure **PADS4 Viewer on the IAdea XMP-8550 / XMP-8552**.

***

### **2.1 Installing PADS4IAdea from USB Stick**

The **PADS4IAdea.apk** file is available on the **PADS4 Server** at:

```
https://[YourPADS4Server]/PADS4/apps  
```

Alternatively, download the latest version from [**here**](https://github.com/PADS4/PADS4-Viewer-IAdea/releases/latest) and place it in the **PADS4 Server apps directory**.

#### **Installation Steps**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Copy the **PADS4IAdea.apk** onto a **USB stick**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Two">
    Insert the **USB stick** into the **IAdea XMP-8550 / XMP-8552** device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Three">
    Open **Basic Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Four">
    Click on **Advanced**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Five">
    Click **three times** in the **lower right corner** of the **Advanced menu** to access Android.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Six">
    The **Android Home Screen** will now be visible.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Seven">
    Open the **File Manager app**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Eight">
    Allow **storage and USB permissions** if prompted.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Nine">
    Click on **Internal Storage** (top left), then select **USB Storage**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Ten">
    Select **PADS4IAdea.apk** and click **Install**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Eleven">
    Once installed, press **Open**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

## **3. Configuring the Device for PADS4IAdea**

To ensure **PADS4 Viewer starts automatically**, update the device settings.

***

### **3.1 Setting Automatic Start**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Step One">
    Open the **PADS4IAdea app** and click on **System Settings**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Step Two">
    Click on **Avancé**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Trois">
    Sélectionnez **Content → AppStart**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Quatre">
    Ouvrez le **sous-menu Advanced**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Cinq">
    Dans **Package Name**, saisissez :

    ```
    com.pads4.viewer.iadea  
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Six">
    Cliquez sur **Set** pour enregistrer le paramètre.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Sept">
    Cliquez sur le **bouton Back** pour revenir aux **Basic Settings**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

### **3.2 Connexion au PADS4 Server**

**Format d'adresse du PADS4 Server :**

```
protocol://host:port/hostpath/  
```

* **Protocol:** `http` ou `https` (dépend du certificat SSL).
* **Host:** Nom DNS ou **adresse IP** du **PADS4 Server**.
* **Port:** *(Optionnel - à spécifier uniquement si vous utilisez des ports non standard).*
* **Chemin d'hôte :** Configuré dans **System Management** *`(default: PADS4)`*.

#### **Étapes de connexion**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Étape Une">
    Saisissez l'**emplacement du PADS4 Server**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Deux - *Optionnel*">
    Spécifiez un **Viewer Name** (si laissé vide, PADS4 en générera un).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Trois">
    Appuyez sur **Start**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Quatre">
    Accordez la **permission de stocker définitivement le contenu** lorsque vous y êtes invité.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Étape Cinq">
    **Chrome se mettra à jour** en arrière-plan, et l'application redémarrera.
  </Step>
</Steps>

***

## **4. Mise à jour de PADS4 Viewer & PADS4IAdea App**

L'application **PADS4 Viewer & PADS4IAdea app** se **mettra à jour automatiquement** lorsque le **PADS4 Server** est mis à jour.

***

## **5. Fonctionnalités et capacités prises en charge**

| Fonctionnalité | Description                                                                                                    |
| :------------: | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|        ✅       | **Mises à jour automatiques du logiciel** – Se met à jour lorsque le **PADS4 Server** est mis à jour.          |
|        ✅       | **Mode hors ligne** – L'appareil continue d'exécuter les présentations pendant **7 jours** en mode hors ligne. |
|        ✅       | **Support HTTP & HTTPS**                                                                                       |
|        ✅       | **Support Cluster Viewer**                                                                                     |
|        ✅       | **Maximum 2 vidéos par page** – Prend en charge **Seamless Video, Rotation, Reflection, Skew, Flip**.          |
|        ❌       | **Streaming Video** – Non pris en charge.                                                                      |
|        ✅       | **Éléments PowerPoint & PDF** – *(PowerPoint nécessite une installation sur le PADS4 Server)*.                 |
|        ✅       | **Gestion des appareils** – Contrôle à distance via **PADS4 CTRL Center & PADS4 Agent**.                       |
|        ✅       | **Redémarrage & Relance du Viewer** – Pris en charge.                                                          |
|        ❌       | **Wake-on-LAN** – Non pris en charge.                                                                          |
|        ✅       | **Suivi Proof of Play** – Surveille l'historique de lecture du contenu.                                        |
