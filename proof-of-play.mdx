---

Proof of Play lets you record detailed playback information for a presentation. The resulting logs are useful when you need to demonstrate to advertisers, customers, or internal stakeholders that specific content was actually displayed.

## What gets logged

When Proof of Play is enabled, PADS4 records:

- **When** the presentation played, including start and end times.
- **Where** it played, identified by viewer and computer.
- **How long** each page or element was shown.

Logs are written locally and sent to the PADS4 Server for centralized reporting.

## Settings

**Log Presentation Statistics Info**

Turn this option on to enable Proof of Play for the presentation. PADS4 stores the playback details in a log file on the viewer and forwards them to the PADS4 Server.
