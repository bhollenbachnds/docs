  <Column>
    **After logging in to Canva, PADS4 opens the Canva Content Library, where all available Canva designs and images are displayed in one overview.** 

    **From this library, you can browse the content, choose the item you want to use, and select the preferred import format before bringing it into PADS4.** 

    **The library supports three import options: Image (JPG) for static visuals, PDF for document-based content, and Video (MP4) for moving content. The Image option loads the first page of the design as an image, the PDF option creates a slideshow from the design pages, and the Video option turns the design into a movie that includes all effects and animations.** 

    **The items are loaded in the Media element for Image and Video, or in the Document element for PDF. Check the available elements to see the possibilities of each one.**

**In addition, you also have access to the images available in the Canva Content Library. Once you have selected the desired format, simply drag and drop the PADS4 Presentation into the workspace to continue.** 

## Editing a Canva Design

You can edit a Canva design directly. Double-click the Canva design in the Canva Content Library. Canva opens in a new browser tab to load your design. You can then make your changes in Canva. When you are ready, return to PADS4 and refresh the Canva Content Library. A thumbnail of your updated design will appear and your presentation will update.
