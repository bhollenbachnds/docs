---
title: "PADS4 Workspace"
---

PADS4 Workspace makes workplace booking simple and reliable. People can reserve a room or a desk in one place, while the system helps keep the workplace organized and easy to use.

It also goes beyond basic booking. Rooms can be matched to the equipment they need, ghost bookings can be prevented, and services like food and beverage can be handled as part of the reservation.

<Frame>
  ![PADS4 Workspace](/images/CTRL-Center/PADS4-Workspace.png)
</Frame>

That means employees only see rooms that really work for their meeting, because broken equipment is left out. In practice, PADS4 Workspace helps teams book faster, avoid wasted space, and run meetings more smoothly.

For a more detailed guide, see the PADS4 Workspace manual.
