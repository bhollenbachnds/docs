title: "Certified Viewer Devices"

A Viewer device, also known as a Player, is a hardware unit used to display Digital Signage presentations.

This section lists all certified PADS4 Viewer devices, arranged in alphabetical order. Each listed device is thoroughly tested with every new PADS4 release to ensure full compatibility and reliable performance.
