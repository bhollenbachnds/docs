---
title: "Connect PADS4 Designer to Canva"
description: "Authenticate PADS4 Designer with Canva from the toolbar to start importing Canva designs and assets into your digital signage presentations."
---

1. Open PADS4 Design to start configuring the Canva integration.
   <Frame>
     ![App Laucher](/images/DigitalSignage/Designer/AppLaucher.png)
   </Frame>
   Create a new presentation or open an existing one in which you want to use Canva content.
2. Click the **Canva** icon in the toolbar. 
3. Select **Sign in with Canva** to begin the authentication process.
   <Frame>
     <img
       src="/images/DigitalSignage/Designer/Canva.png"
       alt="Canva"
       title="Canva"
     />
   </Frame>
4. A prompt will appear to create a new Canva app registration. 
   <Frame>
     ![New Canva Registration](/images/DigitalSignage/Designer/NewCanvaRegistration.png)
   </Frame>
5. Enter a clear and recognizable name for the integration to identify it later if needed.
6. Enter the **App (Client) ID** and **Client Secret** that were generated earlier in the Canva Developer Portal.
7. Click **Create** to register the integration within PADS4.\
8. An overview screen will appear displaying the Canva account and credentials being used. Review the details and click **Allow** to grant PADS4 access to your Canva content.
   <Frame>
     ![Canva Logon PADS4](/images/DigitalSignage/Designer/CanvaLogonPADS4.png)
   </Frame>
9. After successful authorization, your Canva designs will be displayed in the left-hand panel, allowing you to browse and select content directly within Designer.
10. The Canva integration is now fully configured and ready for use in your presentation.\
